Marketr

Marketr is an AI bot designed to function as a Marketing Assistant. Simply provide it with a brief description of a campaign and specify the number of posts you need. With a single click, it can generate hundreds or even thousands of unique social media posts, with accompanying hashtags and emojis.

Posting valuable and relevant content just became effortless

You have the flexibility to adjust the frequency and select from a wide range of social media platforms that align with your business and industry. Additionally, each post can be paired with a graphic or picture.

AI generated images or your own stock collection

You can either upload stock photos to accompany these posts or utilize our Stable Diffusion model to create graphics based on the post using AI image generation.
